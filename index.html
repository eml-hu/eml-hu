Welcome to the Energy Materials Lab.

Research in the Energy Materials Lab. focuses on developing materials for polymer electrolyte membrane fuel cells (PEMFCs), solid-oxide fuel cells (SOFCs), and dye-sensitized solar cells (DSSCs). Navigate to RESEARCH and PUBLICATIONS for detail information about our activities.
